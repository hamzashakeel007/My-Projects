BMI Interpretation
Underweight (BMI below 18.5)

Consult a healthcare professional for potential health risks.

Healthy Weight (BMI 18.5-24.9)

Aim for a balanced diet and regular activity.

Overweight (BMI 25-29.9)

Consider lifestyle changes (diet, exercise) to improve health.

Obese (BMI 30 or above)

Increased risk of health problems. Consult a healthcare professional for guidance and support.



What is Body Mass Index (BMI)?

  • A simple calculation of weight in relation to height.
  • Calculated as BMI = weight (kg) / height (mÂ²).
  • A general indicator of weight status, but not a perfect measure of health.
  • Limitations: doesn't account for muscle mass, bone density, or body composition.
